How does it Work?

Most drone delivery services only use GPS navigation, which holds an accuracy of approximately 8 meters.

We aren't most drone delivery services...

Our drones utilize a Last-Mile navigation technique which uses a binary signal that is generated by a cell phone flash or any other signal medium to provide a point that the drone can lock onto. This technique has been proven to hold an accuracy of less than 0.5 meters.

DronesDash is also developing patent pending authentication techniques to ensure that packages are always delivered to right person.

We can't say too much about it right now, but it is going to be some exciting stuff.

HYDROGEN POWERED?

DronesDash is proud to work with a Renewable Energy Industry leader like Intelligent Energy.

The hydrogen fuel cell technology that Intelligent Energy has developed will increase our drones flight items from minutes to hours, which will extend our delivery ranges to approximately 10 times farther than any other drone company. We expect to complete the integration of their tech onto our system by Q1 of 2017.
