Meet the team

Founders

Chase Traficanti, CEO

Chase built his first drone when he was 19 years old at Santa Clara University. After graduation in 2013, he worked as a product design engineer at Robson Technologies. Throughout his professional life, Chase has contributed to startups as both a consultant and as a Co-Founder. Chase is dedicated to using compassion as a core business ethic which means that he focuses on the triple bottom line: profits, people and planet.

John Gotcher, COO

Having extensive roots in video production, John is no stranger to drones. As president, producer and co-founder at Virtual Earth VR, he acquired experience in nearly every role of a startup. John is committed to positive world change and believes that technology is the best way to achieve a sustainable earth.

Ayush Jain, CTO

From coding at the age of 7, to building robots since the age of 10, Ayush has grown a passion for innovating. He has worked on computer vision applications using optical character recognition to create Q&As from short paragraphs of text. Ayush has also done research on machine learning to understand electromyographic data for use in prosthetics. Ayush is excited to leverage drone technology to positively impact the world.

Shivum Agarwal, CBO

The phrase “age is just a number” applies to Shivum in a strong and meaningful way. Despite being a teenager, Shivum has always tried to step out of this classification and demonstrate his ability. Over the span of 2 years, Shivum has gained experience in Business Development, Market Research, and Data Analysis. Shivum is devoted to creating a brighter future not just for himself but for the rest of the world.
